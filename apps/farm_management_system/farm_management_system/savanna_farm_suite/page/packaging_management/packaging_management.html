Packaging Management

Define packaging items, record stock purchases, configure product packaging and create packaging collections.

1. Packaging Item

Define a packaging container (e.g. 2-pack tray, 6-pack tray). Auto-creates a stock Item.

2. Purchase Stock

Record purchases of packaging items to add stock.

3. Configure Product

Attach packaging items to a product (e.g., Eggs → 2-pack, 6-pack, 12-pack).

4. Package Collection

Pick a product, enter qty per packaging type, save. Stock is deducted on submit.
